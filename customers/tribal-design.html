TRIBAL DESIGN

Industry
Retail

Levi's procurement is going to metric-driven

Our RFID based Item-level inventory tracking solution allowed Levi's to drastically improve store efficiency and inventory accuracy of 98%
Company Overview:
Tribal Design llc is a global sourcing company headquartered in New York .
Headquarters
New York, United States
Employees
11-50
Customer Website
tribal-design.com
 

Challenge

  • The pandemic put a huge amount of stress and limited daily activity for many. Some people gained weight during the pandemic, and many people lost weight. The uprising challenged the Levis procurement program, disrupts the warehouse fulfillment process. Most of the manufacturers were unable to keep the pace to follow the size-set ration program in bulk shipping and found inaccurate SKUs at warehouse. Levis retail fatigued with inaccurate inventory counts for consumers fulfillment.

Solution

  • ITG enabled Levis successful adoption of RFID item-level digital identification in Levis production hub. Our RFID based Item-level inventory tracking solution allowed Levis to drastically improve store efficiency and inventory accuracy of 98%
  • Accurate stock counts are essential to a brands ability to manage their inventory, avoid stockouts, forecast demand, reduce inventory shrinkage rate, and maintain a healthy profit margin.

Result

  • Expanding the user base with supply chain solution set and empowering team of all sizes
  • Leading company through disruptions by evaluating data and planning ahead
  • Positively influencing teams with useful data and solving problems in a real-time

ITG helps us change the way we do supply chain planning. With ITG we have successfully expanded our solution space and our user base.

Mr XYZ, Principle Supply Chain Optimization, Levi's USA
