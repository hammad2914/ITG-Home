Challenge

  • The pandemic put a huge amount of stress and limited daily activity for many. Some people gained weight during the pandemic, and many people lost weight. The uprising challenged the Levi’s procurement program, disrupts the warehouse fulfillment process. Most of the manufacturers were unable to keep the pace to follow the size-set ration program in bulk shipping and found inaccurate SKUs at warehouse. Levi’s retail fatigued with inaccurate inventory counts for consumers fulfillment.

Solution

  • ITG enabled Levi’s successful adoption of RFID item-level digital identification in Levi’s production hub. Our RFID based Item-level inventory tracking solution allowed Levi’s to drastically improve store efficiency and inventory accuracy of 98%
  • Accurate stock counts are essential to a brand’s ability to manage their inventory, avoid stockouts, forecast demand, reduce inventory shrinkage rate, and maintain a healthy profit margin.
