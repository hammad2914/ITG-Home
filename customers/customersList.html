When Groupon was looking for a BSM solution, it wasn't an upgrade of legacy systems. It was from a "level zero" based on manual spreadsheets and invoices. They knew they needed a better deal but didn't want something that would burden employees or IT with complex procurement processes.

As a non-profit credit union, Members 1st was able to leverage various Coupa modules to modernize and streamline their operations, saving their members money and minimizing risk.

Consumer Products

Process Manufacturing

Sonoco, the world’s largest producer of composite cans and tubes, gains clarity from the cloud for complete procurement and AP visibility with Coupa.

Telecommunications

With Coupa, Telenor deployed a more user-friendly procurement system that gave increased visibility into its spend, put an end to bottlenecks, and improved cash flow management.

Technology

With a potential IPO on the horizon, Thumbtack's Josh Waldron utilizes Coupa for compliance and control while empowering employees and suppliers.

Banking & Investment

Industrial Manufacturing
