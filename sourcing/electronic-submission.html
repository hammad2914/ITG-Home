Risk Assessment with ITG

The need to manage supply chain risks consistently and proactively is all time high in today’s world. A supply chain disruption at any point in the process can significantly impact business operations, including disruptions to production, delivery of goods and services, and ultimately, revenue and profit. Organizations can reduce risk with our built-in assessment tool on following core areas
  • Strategic Risk
