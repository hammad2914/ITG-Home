Increase Visibility, Compliance, and Control Across Your Entire Non-Employee Workforce

Companies spend more on services than any other category, but often lack visibility & control. Coupa provides advanced capabilities to source & manage your contingent workers & extended services spend at scale.
  • Enhance Process Efficiency

    Quickly address your business needs by getting the right people onboarded faster and making it easier for managers to track projects to completion.

  • Reduce Compliance Risk

    Protect access to your facilities, protect your IP, and ensure compliance with labor laws and other regulations.

  • Improve Performance and Value

    Get the most value out of every dollar spent on services by sourcing at the lowest rates, tracking work to completion, and closely monitoring performance.

Expedite Worker Tracking Processes

Directly create or upload workers with optional approvals and/or onboarding steps configured specifically for worker tracking use cases. Accelerate your processes for onboarding non-billable headcount tracking resources, while ensuring your resources are properly engaged. Allow contractors to easily enter timesheets and service completion details via email, while enabling seamless collaboration with you throughout the entire lifecycle of a services project.

Enforce Automated Compliance Requirements

Pre-defined onboarding requirements automatically trigger based on requisition and candidate data selections, such as department, commodity, or location, to allow suppliers to take immediate action once an offer has been accepted. Ensure workers are onboarded per legal and business requirements by completing compliance and document tasks.

SOW: Functionality for Advanced Requests

Through a series of questions, Coupa's Guided Workflow & Engagement Toolkit helps drive users to the proper requisition and allows workers to be classified and onboarded according to defined legal and business processes.
