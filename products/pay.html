Optimize Your Financial Health Across Spend, Payments, and Liquidity

You can’t control what you can’t see. Organizational and system silos create blind spots. Coupa Pay banishes these blind spots by giving you the complete picture into your spend, payments, and liquidity so you can make smarter decisions faster.
  • Improve Operational Efficiency

    Improve efficiency of time consuming processes with touchless invoicing and automatic digital payments using any payment method, including virtual cards. Reduce effort by automating cash management and settlement processes across multiple entities and accounts.

  • Optimize Financial Performance

    Drive working capital by maximizing return on cash, increasing Days Payable Outstanding (DPO), or reducing your borrowing costs.

  • Increase Control and Reduce Risk

    Gain greater control of your spend, payments, and cash flows. Mitigate error, fraud, and regulatory compliance risk due to manual processes and disparate systems, and hedge financial risks, such as FX and interest volatility.

Streamline Supplier Management

Supplier Management automates supplier onboarding and ensures accurate collection of supplier payment details. If suppliers deactivate their Remit-to's in the CSP, it will automatically get deactivated in Coupa Pay. Coupa Working Capital provides a unified experience for suppliers to manage and submit invoices for early payment within their invoicing flow. Buyers using working capital products can leverage a seamless experience to manage invoices eligible for early payment and perform reconciliations during the payment process.
Explore Supplier Information Management

Payments + Spend Guard + Risk Aware

Prevent payment of potentially fraudulent invoices and leverage the power of Community Intelligence and other external data sources to identify high risk suppliers.
Learn More

Take Control of Risk and Fraud

Coupa Pay's integration of Spend Guard and Risk Aware findings hardens your payment security controls and helps your payment professionals focus on the most risky and questionable submissions. With alerts on suspicious transactions that should be reviewed prior to batching and payment, Coupa helps prevent payments to potentially fraudulent invoices. Supplier risk categories powered Coupa Pay and Risk Aware allow you to further exercise appropriate caution with certain suppliers before executing business transactions.
